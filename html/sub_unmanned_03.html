UUV6000

UUV6000 is a deep-sea unmanned submersible with the capacity of exploring 95% of the entire oceans on earth. It is a robotic system that conducts exploratory mission using a robot arm in an extreme condition of ultra-pressure and complete darkness under deep sea beyond human access.
This equipment enabled us to commence exploration of marine resources in deep sea of East Sea and Pacific Ocean since 2006, which helped to obtain recognition that Korea is the fourth country in the world that retain a submarine with the capability to explore the sea 6,000m deep.

Applications

  • Scientific Research
  • Physical, Biological, and Geological Oceanography Investigation
  • Survey of Ocean Resources
  • Inspection of Underwater Structure & Ship Wreckage
  • Infrastructure for Deep-sea Development
  • Design & Performance Evaluation for Deep Sea Instruments

Hybrid ROV

H-ROV is a two-man-portable hovering ROV designed for ship hull inspection. H-ROV uses thrusters to hover, rotate in place and translate in any direction, enabling operations in confined areas.

Applications

  • Ship Hull & Infrastructure Inspection
  • Port & Harbor Security
  • Mine Countermeasures (MCM)
  • Scientific Research

Sea Rover R100

Sea Rover R100 is designed for surveillance and monitoring of environmental ecology in coastal areas

Key Features

  • Automatic dynamic control
  • Underwater hybrid navigation algorithm
  • Underwater communication system

ROV 400

ROV 400 is designed for surveillance and inspection of underwater structure with survival depth 400 meters

Key Features

  • Inspection of underwater structure
  • Bridge Inspection and port security
  • Embedded automatic water depth detection
  • Azimuth controlling function

Aqua Farm Monitoring ROV

Aqua Farm Monitoring is specially for maritime farm monitoring abnormal marine environment

Key Features

  • Aquaculture/fishery aquatic environment management
  • Underwater environmental data monitoring
  • Autonomous moving around a floating net cage
  • Clearing and sweeping garbage from the fisheries
