CIS (Combined Influence Sweeping)

The CIS System combines both magnetic and acoustic minesweeping capabilities. Magnetic minesweeping involves creating a magnetic field in the water that replicates those of a ship to safely detonate magnetic mines at a distance. Acoustic minesweeping involves replicating the acoustic signature of ships to safely detonate acoustic mines at a distance.
